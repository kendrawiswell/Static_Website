Humble Cup Coffee

Humble Cup is a local coffee shop close to downtown Minneapolis. Located in Seven Corners near West Bank of the University of Minnesota Campus, we serve Truestone coffee and espresso, a variety of bakery items, house made waffles, sandwiches, and salads!

Truestone Coffee

Our coffee comes from True Stone Coffee, a local coffee roaster with the highest quality coffee beans that are extraordinarily fresh.
